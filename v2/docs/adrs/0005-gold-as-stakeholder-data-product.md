# ADR 0005: Gold Models Are Stakeholder Data Products

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Portfolio pipelines often stop at technical transformations without demonstrating business impact or decision utility.

## Decision
Gold models are framed as governed stakeholder-facing metrics (engagement, moderation risk, sentiment, participation behavior), not generic technical aggregates.

## Consequences
- Improves business relevance and communication with non-engineering consumers.
- Encourages semantic stability, ownership, and documentation discipline.
- Requires ongoing metric governance and explicit definition changes.
